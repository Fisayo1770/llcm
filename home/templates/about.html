OUR MISSION
Brethren, there is no small vision or ministry in life, neither is there any muster seed without a potential to become a great tree but it is clearly the man inputs that determines his/her outputs. That is, if show sparingly receives sparingly and bountifully receives bountifully then we must endeavor without holding anything back give our all to God and the interest of His kingdom as laid down by our Lord Jesus Christ in order to have a good result both as a ministry and individual. 2 Corinthians 9:6 Galatians 6:7
What then is God’s purpose for this ministry? For if purpose for a cause is not known then the abuse is unavoidable.
  • The purpose for this ministry is one, just as the coming of Jesus Christ to the world was based on one se that is to save the whole world. (John 3:17)
